# Personas — Workspace activity digest

## Primary

### PM (Product Manager)
- Tracks ~6 squads at a time.
- Cares about decisions, scope changes, and customer-impacting
  events surfaced in <10 minutes.
- Pain: scrolling channels and threads to reconstruct what
  happened overnight.

### EM (Engineering Manager)
- Tracks 1–2 squads deeply, plus cross-team dependencies.
- Cares about deploys, incidents, blocked PRs.
- Pain: signal/noise ratio in shared channels.

## Secondary
- Designers, on-call engineers — get the digest but are not the
  primary audience.
