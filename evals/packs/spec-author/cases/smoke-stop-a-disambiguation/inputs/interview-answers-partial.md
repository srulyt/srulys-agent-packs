# Scripted interview answers — partial

These answers cover most P0 questions but deliberately leave the
rollout-risk question unanswered to exercise the C5 partial-answer
fallback.

## Problem Statement
- Q (P0): What user pain are we solving? — Members miss important
  changes across the workspace; scrolling individual channels takes
  too long.

## Goals & Success Metrics
- Q (P0): What outcome metric? — Reduce median time-to-first-action
  on a flagged change from 45 minutes to under 10 minutes for >70%
  of digest recipients within 90 days post-launch.

## Users & Personas
- Q (P0): Who are the primary users? — Product managers and
  engineering managers who own one or more workspaces.

## Solution Summary
- Q (P0): What's the rough shape? — A daily in-app notification at
  user-configurable time summarising changes in followed workspaces.
