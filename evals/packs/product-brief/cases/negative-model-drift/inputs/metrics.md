## Cohort splits

- **SMB (1–10 technicians):** 44% of accounts, 22% of ARR.
  Dispatcher cognitive load is less acute here (often the same
  person who wears another hat).
- **Mid-market (11–80 technicians):** 41% of accounts, 51% of ARR.
  Dispatcher cognitive load is the dominant complaint.
- **Enterprise (>80 technicians):** 15% of accounts, 27% of ARR.
  Reporting export and customer-facing visibility dominate.
