# Polariq KPIs — relevant to A-vs-B prioritisation

| Metric | Current | FY26 target | Feature A impact | Feature B impact |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mid-market trial→paid conversion | 19.2% | 26% | +3 to +5 pp | none expected |
| Enterprise win rate (closed RFPs) | 31% | 40% | none expected | +6 to +9 pp |
| Enterprise gross retention | 92.4% | ≥ 94% | none expected | +1 to +2 pp |
| SMB trial→paid conversion | 14.8% | 18% | none | none |
| New ARR (mid-market mix) | 38% | 40% | reinforces | none |
| New ARR (enterprise mix) | 22% | 26% | none | reinforces |

## ARR-weighted impact (modeled, FY26)

- **Feature A:** +$1.8M to +$3.0M projected new ARR, concentrated in
  mid-market self-serve.
- **Feature B:** +$2.4M to +$4.1M projected ARR (mix of enterprise
  net-new wins and renewal protection), concentrated in enterprise.

## Cost envelope

- Feature A: ~$640K fully loaded (4 months, Pipeline pod).
- Feature B: ~$880K fully loaded (5 months, Forecasting pod, plus
  data-platform dependency).

## Decision context

The PM lead has asked for a recommendation between A and B, with
explicit weighting of the segment trade-off (mid-market growth vs.
enterprise risk reduction) and the dependency-bottleneck constraint
(only one of the two can ship in H1 FY26).
