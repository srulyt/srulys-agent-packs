# Proposed Success Metrics

These are the metrics the Activation pod proposes to be measured on
for the FY26 onboarding redesign. They are intended to be visible on
the CFO office's revenue model and on the VP Product's pod scorecard.

## Primary metric

**Trial-to-paid conversion (self-serve, SMB + mid-market)**

- Current: 14.1% (trailing quarter)
- FY24 baseline: 18.4%
- Target at end of FY26: **22%**
- Measurement window: rolling 90-day cohort, measured monthly
  starting one month after GA.
- Why this one: it is the metric that most directly translates into
  the FY26 revenue plan and the one the CFO office is already
  modeling against.

## Supporting metrics

These are leading indicators. They move first; the primary metric
moves later.

**Day-3 activation rate**

- Current: 23%
- Target: 38%
- Activation defined as: at least one recurring data source
  connected AND at least one role-appropriate first task completed
  (dashboard published for analysts, permissions configured for
  admins, first-close milestone started for finance leads).

**Day-30 activation rate**

- Current: 41%
- Target: 60%
- Same definition as above, measured at day 30.

**Median time-to-first-value**

- Current: 6.2 days (on converting trials)
- Target: under 3 days

**Support tickets per net-new account in first 30 days**

- Current: 1.7 tickets / account
- Target: under 1.0 tickets / account
- This is what unlocks the support-capacity offset cited in the
  funding ask.

## Guardrail metrics

We will fail the project if any of these regress materially.

- Net Promoter Score from net-new accounts: must not drop more than
  3 points from current 32.
- Trial signup rate (top of funnel): must not drop. The redesign is
  inside the product, but a heavier first-login experience could in
  principle deter signups; we do not expect this but will watch it.
- First-90-day gross revenue retention on new cohorts: must not
  regress from current 94%.

## What we will not commit to

- Enterprise-segment conversion. Enterprise onboarding is
  human-assisted and out of scope for this work.
- Expansion revenue from existing accounts. The redesign targets
  net-new activation, not existing-account growth.

## Reporting cadence

Monthly to VP Product and CFO office, starting one month post-GA, in
the existing FY26 business review format. No new dashboards required.
