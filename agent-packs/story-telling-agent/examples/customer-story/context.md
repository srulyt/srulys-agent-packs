# Example: Customer Story Deck

**Audience**: prospects in financial-services vertical (mid-market CFOs and their analytics leads), shown by AEs during the second sales call.

**Decision needed**: prospect agrees to a paid pilot.

**Tone**: customer-voice-forward. The deck is mostly *the customer talking*; the vendor brand stays in the background. Quotes drive every section break.

**Time slot**: 20 min, projected on a shared screen during a Zoom call.

**Constraints / known facts**
- Reference customer: Northwind Bank (regional, $4B AUM).
- Champion at Northwind: Director of FP&A (quoted in case study, cleared for external use).
- Outcome metrics (cleared): forecast accuracy +18 pts, close cycle from 9 days → 4 days, three-FTE team unchanged.
- Implementation took 11 weeks; champion called out three risk moments and how they were handled.
- Avoid claims about other customers — this deck is *Northwind's* story.
- Hard rule: every metric slide must be paired with a customer quote on the same or adjacent slide.

**Out of scope**: pricing, contract terms, generic product feature lists.
